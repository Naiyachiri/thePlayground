The Common Raven

Ravens have been found to be intelligent enough to discern gunshots from other loud sounds and seek them in order to find carcasses for food.

  • Scientific Name: Corvus Corax
  • Average Length: 56-69cm (22-27.2 in)
  • Average Lifespan: 10-15 Years
  • Habitat: North America and spanning down the west coast to central Mexico

Jet black and varying in size, ravens are among the smartest birds and are capable of problem solving and adaptation. Ravens engage in teenage gang activity and have been known to overwhelm adult ravens and steal their food. For more information click here!
