So it begins... A blogger who tells his story about growing and doing what he likes

March 9, 2018

So today is a corner stone in my life of trying to figure out what I want and like to do. I have always had an upbringing in which I tend to follow the guidelines and desires of my elders. Be a doctor, make money to help the family, work, work, work... Rare was the idea of what one likes or is interested ever explored in the household.

Reflecting upon my life, "Have I lived my own life or just spent it trying to please everyone." is what bobs in my mind like a buoy. Its funny how our minds are geared to seeing patterns and correlations.
